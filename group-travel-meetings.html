Myer Hospitality Group Travel & Meetings

Picking a travel destination is more than finding a great hotel. It’s also about finding great things to do once you get there – or better yet – before you get there. Myer Hospitality knows as much about entertainment as it does lodging. When you’re a guest at one of our award winning hotels, we can arrange activities at our Segway Adventure Park, book event tickets from the on-site ticket office in all our hotels and make sure you know about the best deals on the wide variety of dining options.  Whatever brings your group to Branson, we’ll help make it memorable.
